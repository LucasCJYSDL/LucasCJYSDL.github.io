I have worked as a research assistant on autonomous driving techniques from 2018/09 to 2020/07, under the supervision of Prof. Huijing Zhao, and completed my Bachelor thesis there. 

Here are the research projects completed during this time:
- Supervised Learning for semantic segmentation of 3D LiDAR data collected with self-driving vehicles.
- Semantic segmentation of 3D LiDAR data in dynamic scenes using Semi-Supervised Learning.
- V2X behavior learning for autonomous vehicles through Reinforcement Learning and Imitation Learning based on the CARLA simulator.
- Bachelor Thesis: Autonomous Driving Control for Interaction with Pedestrains based on Imitation Learing
